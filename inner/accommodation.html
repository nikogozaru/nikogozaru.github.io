Accommodations

This is a list of accommodations where kitchens are available and halal cookingware is offered for Muslim guests. You can order food from the Osaka Masjid and have them delivered to any of these placed on the first day you arrive in Japan. The kitchens are kept clean and haram foods are prohibited. You cook in these places with an easy mind.

Shinsaibashi - Osaka

Shinsaibashi Gallary House-4 Rooms This is a very good property for large groups. There are four separate apartments each equiped with a small kitchen, shower, toilet and pocket wifi. There are a total of 4 double beds and 4 sofa beds allowing a maximum of 16 guests at a time. The separate apartments make it easy to allow families to stay separately or for girls to stay separately from boys. The owner provides special Halal cooking sets and kitchen ware upon request. The building is centeraly located close to the major Shinsaibashi Shopping street with many halal restaurants close by. It is easy to arrive from the airport and the building is easy to find.
