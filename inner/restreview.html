Restaurant Reviews

Make sure to click on the name of the restaurant to get more information fro their website and the location to see a map. We would love to hear your feedback.

Honmachi Mughal is a Pakastani styled restaurant in the heart of Osaka, near the Honmachi subway station. Here is some something about the menu, ambiance and the service.

Shama Horie

Shama is a vegitarian Indian restaurant just outside the Amemura area just outside the south end of the Yotsubashi Subway Station. The B1 level restaurant is small but homey. The service is good and the food is authentic Indian. They donate a percentage of thier profits to support orphanages in India.
